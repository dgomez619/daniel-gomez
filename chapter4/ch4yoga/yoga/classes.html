Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginnerd and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minutes class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana proper alignment and posture), pranayamaa (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your ming and body connection
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermidiate to advance students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focues on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energitcally through yoga poses
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are suppoerted by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level or experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury
